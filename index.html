Company philosophy

It is proposed to use tea to create "Human Shushu" and "Green Shucha". The tea production and processing momentum is strong, the tea garden management is standardized, and the selection and promotion of fine varieties are strengthened.。 　 　
The first is to take the xx tea field as the leader and focus on creating Shu tea tea brands. XX Tea Farm invested 50,000 yuan to purchase and install 60 cubic meters of tea fresh-keeping warehouse, and purchased a set of 70-type tea killing machines and a single tea tree pruning machine, and put them into use. In order to facilitate the traffic in the park, 300 meters of roads in the park were renovated. XX Tea Farm is committed to the production of famous and superior teas. This year, a total of 5.5 tons of famous and superior teas were produced and sold, with an output value of more than 1 million yuan.
